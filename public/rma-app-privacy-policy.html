About This App

Rocky Mountain Arsenal History was developed as a school project. It is provided completely free of charge and will never be monetized in any way. The App contains no advertisements, in-app purchases, or hidden revenue models.

Information We Collect

  • The App itself does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information automatically, including but not limited to:

1. Google Play Services

Google Play may collect device identifiers, crash logs, diagnostics, and usage data as part of app distribution.
You can review Google’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Map Tile Providers

When you view maps, your device may request map tiles or geospatial data from servers operated by providers such as Mapbox or MapTiler. These providers may collect non-personal information such as IP address, device type, and usage statistics.

  • Mapbox Privacy Policy: https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy
  • MapTiler Privacy Policy: https://www.maptiler.com/privacy-policy/

Data Sharing
